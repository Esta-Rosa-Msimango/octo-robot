What is the App Factory?

The App Factory Internship Programme is a partnership between Samsung South Africa and Wits University’s Tshimologong Digital Innovation Precinct.

The App Factory is an essential learning ground for teaching the language of technology, which is coding. Interns get to work with experienced senior developers over nine months to grow their competence and hone their skills – an education that can translate into a successful career in a rapidly expanding industry.

This is an immense platform for empowering deserving, previously disadvantaged youth. Aligned with our global mission of ‘Enabling People’, from here they can launch careers that place them at the forefront of the 4th Industrial Revolution.

Solution for the cloud in South Africa

Cloud services have been expanding in popularity across the world, however, it has been the rise in South Africa, Due to the load-shedding that makes it difficult for businesses and institutions to get servers on-premise to work efficiently, and the hardware also becomes more prone to disaster. This makes cloud services an ideal solution within South Africa, within many businesses investing in both private and public cloud services. Private cloud services are limited to one organization, public cloud computing service is shared between organizations, allowing the fees associated with them to be shared.
